Stan Lee

The Real SuperHero Of People

Here's a time line of Stan Lee's life:

  • 1922 - Born in Manhattan, New York City
  • 1939 - Graduated from high school and joined WPA Federal Theatre Project
  • 1945 - Lived in rented top floor of Brownstone
  • 1947 - Married Joan Clayton Boocock
  • 1949 - Bought a house in Woodmere
  • 1952 - Resides in Hewlett Harbor, NY
  • 1960 - Pioneers in superhero comics
  • 1970 - Challenged the restrictions of Comic Code Authority
  • 1975 - Owned a Condominium in Manhattan
  • 1980 - Bought a home in West Hollywood
  • 1981 - Pursued development of Marvel Properties in medias
  • 1981 - Donated portions of his personal effects to University of Wyoming
  • 1990 - Retires from Marvel, still remained a public figure
  • 1994 - Was inducted in comic book industry's Will Eisner Award hall of fame
  • 1995 - Was inducted in comic book industry's Jack Kirby hall of fame
  • 1998 - Set up Stan Lee Entertainment
  • 2008 - Received NEA's National Medal of Arts
  • 2010 - Stan Lee foundation was founded
  • 2012 - A pacemaker was inserted through an operation
  • 2017 - Retired from convention appearance
  • 2018 - Passed away on November 12, at the age of 95
